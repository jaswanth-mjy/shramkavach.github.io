Frequently Asked Questions

Is ShramKavach really free?

Yes, 100% free. All our calculators, document generators, AI prompts, and guides cost nothing to use. We sustain through advertising.

Do you store my data?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Are the calculators accurate?

Our calculators are based on official formulas and current tax/labour laws. However, they provide estimates — always verify important calculations with a qualified professional. See our Disclaimer.

Can I use the legal document templates?

Yes, our templates are free to use. They are designed for general use and comply with Indian law. We recommend having complex legal documents reviewed by a qualified attorney before using them in formal settings.

How often are tools updated?

We update our calculators and guides whenever there are changes to Indian tax laws, labour codes, or social security regulations. Most tools are current for the 2025-26 financial year.
