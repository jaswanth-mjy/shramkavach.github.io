GST Threshold Raised to ₹40 Lakhs

SK

ShramKavach Editorial Team

Labour Law & Tax Experts • Verified Content

Confirmed in Budget 2026! GST registration threshold ₹20 lakh → ₹40 lakh doubled हो गया है general category states के लिए। Special category states के लिए ₹10 → ₹20 lakh। Effective April 1, 2026। Small businesses, freelancers, और part-time workers को huge compliance relief मिलेगी!

📢 GST Registration Threshold Doubled

❌ Old Rules (Till March 31, 2026)

  • • General states: ₹20 lakh turnover
  • • Special states: ₹10 lakh turnover
  • • Mandatory registration above limit
  • • Monthly/quarterly return filing

✅ New Rules (From April 1, 2026)

  • • General states: ₹40 lakh turnover
  • • Special states: ₹20 lakh turnover
  • • No GST registration if below limit
  • • No compliance burden for small businesses

🎉 Who Benefits Most?

💼

Freelancers

Writers, designers, developers earning ₹20-40L → No GST compliance needed!

🚗

Gig Workers

Cab drivers, delivery partners with side businesses → threshold doubled

🏠

Home Businesses

Homemade products, online sellers → ₹40L तक GST-free growth

🗺️ State-wise GST Threshold

🌇 General Category States

₹40 Lakh

Annual turnover limit (goods)

Includes:

• Maharashtra, Gujarat, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu

• Delhi, UP, Rajasthan, MP, West Bengal

• Punjab, Haryana, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh

(27 states + 7 UTs)

🏔️ Special Category States

₹20 Lakh

Annual turnover limit (goods)

Includes (11 states):

• North-East: Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, Tripura

• Hilly States: Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand

• Islands: Jammu & Kashmir

⚠️ Services vs Goods:

Goods: ₹40L threshold (general) / ₹20L (special)
Services: ₹20L threshold (ALL states)—यह unchanged रहेगा

📊 Impact on Gig Workers & Small Businesses

✅ Benefits

  • No GST compliance: Return filing, invoice rules, tax payments—सब से छुट्टी (if under ₹40L)
  • Cost savings: CA fees (₹2-3K/month), GST software (₹1K/month)—yearly ₹36-48K saved
  • Simplified pricing: No need to add 18% GST in quotations—competitive edge
  • Focus on business: Time spent on compliance → business growth में invest करें

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Voluntary registration: ₹40L से कम पर भी GST le sakte हैं (ITC claim के लिए)
  • Inter-state supply: E-commerce sellers के लिए GST mandatory (even under ₹40L)
  • Monitor turnover: ₹40L cross होते ही 30 days में register करना mandatory

🧩 Should You Take Voluntary GST Registration?

✅ Register GST if:

  • You buy raw materials/services with GST (can claim ITC—Input Tax Credit)
  • Your clients are GST-registered businesses (they prefer GST invoices)
  • You plan to scale beyond ₹40L soon (better to start early)
  • You sell on e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Flipkart require GST)

❌ Avoid GST if:

  • You serve individual consumers (B2C business—no ITC benefit for them)
  • You have minimal expenses with GST (no ITC to claim)
  • Your turnover is stable under ₹40L (compliance burden not worth it)
