# Project Shram

India's 150M+ gig workers are the backbone of our economy, yet they lack basic protections and support systems.

We're building ShramKavach - an open-source platform to empower these workers with:

  • ‚Üí Real-time earnings tracking
  • ‚Üí Fair wage calculations
  • ‚Üí Worker rights information
  • ‚Üí Community support networks
