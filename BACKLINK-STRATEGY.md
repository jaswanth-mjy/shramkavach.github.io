## Backlinking Strategy

### How to Use for SEO

1. **Internal Linking**
   - Link from homepage to year-end-2025.html
   - Link from calculators to freelancer-tax-2025.html
   - Cross-link articles in "Related Articles" sections
   - Breadcrumbs provide automatic linking

2. **External Backlinks**
   - Share standalone URLs on social media
   - Submit to article directories
   - Guest post on tax/freelancer blogs with backlinks
   - Answer Quora/Reddit questions with article links
   - Create infographics linking to articles

3. **Content Distribution**
   - LinkedIn posts with article links
   - Twitter threads summarizing articles
   - Email newsletter with article links
   - WhatsApp groups (share button provided)

4. **Google Indexing**
   - Submit both URLs to Google Search Console
   - Request immediate indexing for both pages
   - Monitor in Search Console > Coverage report
   - Check for rich results (Article schema should trigger)
