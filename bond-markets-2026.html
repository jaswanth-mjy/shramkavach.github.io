üìú Three Golden Rules for Bond Investors

1

Embrace a Laddering Strategy

Buy bonds with staggered maturities (1-year, 2-year, 3-year) to hedge against both rising and falling rates.

Benefit: Continuous reinvestment opportunities at prevailing rates

2

Realize Capital Gains

Don't "fall in love" with your bonds. Sell them when you achieve desired gains.

Remember: Profits only matter when you exit!

3

Short-End Bonds if Unsure

For conservative investors: Buy 1-2 year bonds and roll them over.

Advantage: Less sensitive to rate fluctuations, predictable returns
