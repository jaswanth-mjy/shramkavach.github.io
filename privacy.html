Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

ShramKavach ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT store any personal data. All calculations and document generation happen entirely in your browser (client-side). Your data never reaches our servers.

  • Calculator inputs are processed locally in your browser
  • Generated documents are created on your device
  • No forms require personal information submission
  • No login or registration required

3. Analytics & Cookies

We may use:

  • Google Analytics: To understand website usage and improve user experience
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements
  • Essential Cookies: For basic website functionality

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Tailwind CSS CDN (styling)
  • Google Fonts (typography)
  • Cloudflare CDN (jsPDF library)

5. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Use browser privacy/incognito mode
  • Disable JavaScript (though this will break site functionality)

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, please visit our home page.
