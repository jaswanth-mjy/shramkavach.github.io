‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the overnight shift calculator work?

Our calculator automatically detects when your end time is earlier than your start time (e.g., 11 PM to 3 AM) and adds 24 hours to calculate the correct duration. This ensures accurate hour tracking for night shifts and graveyard shifts.

Is my work hour data saved?

Yes! All your work entries are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. Your data persists even if you close the browser or refresh the page. However, clearing browser data will remove your entries.

Can I track multiple jobs?

Absolutely! You can add unlimited work entries with different hourly rates. Use the description field to label entries by job name, making it easy to track hours and earnings across multiple positions.

How is overtime calculated?

Overtime is automatically calculated as any hours worked beyond 40 hours per week. The overtime hours are displayed in the summary cards at the top of the calculator for easy reference.

What format is the CSV export?

The CSV export includes all your work entries with columns for Date, Start Time, End Time, Break Minutes, Hourly Rate, Hours Worked, Earnings, and Description. This format is compatible with Excel, Google Sheets, and other spreadsheet applications.
