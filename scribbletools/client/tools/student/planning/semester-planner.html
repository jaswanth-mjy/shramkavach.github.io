About Our Semester Planner

Our Semester Planner is a comprehensive tool designed to help students organize their academic life. Whether you're in high school, college, or university, managing multiple courses, assignments, and exams can be overwhelming. Our planner simplifies this process by providing a centralized platform to track all your academic commitments.

With our semester planner, you can easily add courses, track assignment deadlines, schedule study sessions, and monitor your academic progress throughout the semester. The intuitive interface allows you to visualize your entire semester at a glance, helping you identify busy periods and plan your time effectively.

Key Features

Course Management

Add and organize all your courses with details like instructor, schedule, credits, and room number.

Assignment Tracking

Keep track of all assignments, projects, and homework with due dates and priority levels.

Exam Scheduling

Schedule all your exams, midterms, and finals with reminders to ensure you're always prepared.

Grade Tracking

Monitor your grades and GPA throughout the semester to stay on top of your academic performance.

Deadline Reminders

Set up notifications for upcoming deadlines to never miss an important date again.

Study Groups

Organize and coordinate study groups with classmates for collaborative learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the semester planner free to use?
Yes, our semester planner is completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible tools to help students succeed academically without financial barriers.
Can I access my planner on multiple devices?
Currently, the planner saves data locally on your browser. We're working on cloud synchronization features that will allow you to access your planner across multiple devices in the future.
Can I export my semester plan?
Yes, you can export your semester plan as a PDF or print it directly from your browser. This feature is perfect for sharing with advisors or keeping a physical copy.
Is my data secure?
Your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We prioritize your privacy and only collect minimal analytics to improve our service.
