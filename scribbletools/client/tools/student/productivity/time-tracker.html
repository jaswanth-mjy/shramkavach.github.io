About Time Tracker

Our Time Tracker is a comprehensive productivity tool designed specifically for students to monitor study sessions, track tasks, and analyze time management patterns. Whether you're preparing for exams, working on assignments, or managing multiple projects, this tool helps you stay focused and productive.

Key Features

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor your study sessions in real-time with precise tracking down to the second. Perfect for understanding exactly how you spend your study time.

Productivity Analytics

Get detailed insights into your daily productivity patterns, including total time spent, tasks completed, and average session lengths.

Task Categorization

Organize your work by category - study sessions, assignments, research, reading, and more. See where your time goes across different activities.

Target Duration

Set target durations for tasks and receive notifications when you reach your goals. Stay motivated and track completion rates.

Pomodoro Ready

Quick Pomodoro button sets up a 25-minute focus session instantly. Perfect for implementing proven productivity techniques.

Auto-Save & Export

All your data is automatically saved locally. Export your session history anytime for analysis or record-keeping.

How to Use Time Tracker

1

Set Your Task

Enter a descriptive task name, select the appropriate category (study, assignment, research, etc.), and optionally set a target duration in minutes.

2

Start Tracking

Click the "Set Current Task" button, then hit "Start" to begin tracking. The timer displays hours:minutes:seconds and updates in real-time.

3

Monitor Progress

Watch the progress bar fill up if you've set a target duration. Use pause/resume functionality for breaks without losing your progress.

4

Complete & Analyze

Click "Stop" when finished. Your session is automatically saved and added to today's analytics. View total time, tasks completed, and session history.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my time tracking data saved?

A: Yes! All your session data is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. It persists between sessions and remains private on your device. You can export your data anytime as a JSON file.

Q: What's the Quick Pomodoro feature?

A: The Quick Pomodoro button instantly sets up a 25-minute focus session, based on the popular Pomodoro Technique. It's perfect for quick study sprints without manual configuration.

Q: Can I pause and resume tracking?

A: Absolutely! Use the Pause button to temporarily stop tracking during breaks, then click Start again to resume from where you left off. Your total time remains accurate.

Q: What happens when I reach my target duration?

A: When you reach your target, you'll receive a visual notification celebrating your achievement. The timer automatically stops and the session is marked as "completed" in your analytics.

Q: How can I view my productivity history?

A: The "Today's Analytics" section shows your daily stats including total time, tasks completed, average session length, and a list of recent sessions. You can also export all your data for deeper analysis.

Q: Is this tool free to use?

A: Yes, completely free! No registration, no hidden costs, no limits. All features are available to all users. Your data stays on your device for complete privacy.
