Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Academic Performance Dashboard work? ‚ñº

The dashboard allows you to input your grades and courses, then automatically calculates your GPA, generates trend charts, and provides performance analytics. It stores your data locally in your browser, so your information remains private and accessible anytime.

Is my academic data secure and private? ‚ñº

Yes, absolutely! All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your grades, GPA, and performance data remain completely private and under your control.

Can I track multiple semesters and academic years? ‚ñº

Yes! The dashboard supports tracking unlimited semesters and academic years. You can view historical performance, compare different periods, and see your overall academic progression over time.

What grading systems does it support? ‚ñº

The dashboard supports various grading systems including letter grades (A, B, C, etc.), percentage-based grading, and custom GPA scales (4.0, 5.0, etc.). You can configure it according to your institution's grading system.

Can I export my performance data? ‚ñº

Yes, you can export your complete performance data including grades, GPA history, and analytics in JSON format. This allows you to keep backups, transfer data between devices, or share your academic records.

How are GPA predictions calculated? ‚ñº

GPA predictions use your historical performance trends and current semester progress to forecast likely outcomes. The predictions become more accurate as you input more data and complete more semesters.

Can I track extracurricular activities and achievements? ‚ñº

The dashboard primarily focuses on academic performance (grades and GPA), but you can add notes and comments to your courses to document achievements, projects, and other relevant information.

Does it work on mobile devices? ‚ñº

Yes! The dashboard is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can track and view your academic performance on any device with a modern web browser.

Can I share my dashboard with others? ‚ñº
