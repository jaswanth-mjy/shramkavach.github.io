How to Use

  1. Upload Your Image:
    • Drag & drop your photo into the upload area
    • Or click "Choose File" to browse
    • High-resolution images work best
  2. Draw Your Selection:
    • Click "Start Drawing" to begin
    • Draw around the area you want to keep
    • Close the shape to complete selection
    • Use "Reset" to start over if needed
  3. Preview & Save:
    • Check the live preview below
    • Click "Finish Crop" when satisfied
    • Download your cropped image
