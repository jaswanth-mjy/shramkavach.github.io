One Rep Max Estimator

About the One Rep Max Estimator

Calculate your estimated One Rep Maximum (1RM) using multiple proven formulas. Enter the weight you lifted and the number of repetitions to get your theoretical maximum single rep.

Formulas Used:
  • Epley: Weight × (1 + 0.0333 × reps)
  • Brzycki: Weight × (36 / (37 - reps))
  • Lander: Weight × (100 / (101.3 - 2.67123 × reps))
  • Lombardi: Weight × reps^0.10

Use Cases:
  • Plan your strength training programs
  • Track progress in powerlifting or weightlifting
  • Set percentage-based training loads
  • Compare strength levels across different exercises
