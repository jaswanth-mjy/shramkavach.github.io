About the Pregnancy Tracker

Instantly track your pregnancy progress, current week, and estimated due date. Enter the first day of your last period to see your pregnancy timeline. Great for expectant parents, planning, or learning about pregnancy.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Track your pregnancy week by week
  • Estimate your due date
  • Plan for appointments and milestones
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
