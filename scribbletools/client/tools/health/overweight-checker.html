About the Overweight Status Analyzer

Instantly check if your BMI is in the overweight or obese range. Enter your weight and height to see your BMI and risk category. Great for health, wellness, or learning about healthy weight.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Check if you are at risk for overweight or obesity
  • Track your BMI for health and wellness
  • Compare BMI for different ages or heights
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
