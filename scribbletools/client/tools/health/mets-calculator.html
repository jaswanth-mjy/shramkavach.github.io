METs Activity Calculator

About the METs Activity Calculator

Calculate calories burned and energy expenditure using METs (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) values for various activities. METs represent the ratio of energy expended during activity to resting metabolic rate.

METs Intensity Levels:
  • Light: 1.5-3.0 METs (walking, light housework)
  • Moderate: 3.0-6.0 METs (brisk walking, dancing)
  • Vigorous: 6.0+ METs (running, competitive sports)

Use Cases:
  • Track calories burned for weight management
  • Compare energy expenditure across activities
  • Plan exercise intensity for fitness goals
  • Monitor daily activity levels
