About the Alcohol Level (BAC) Estimator

Instantly estimate your blood alcohol concentration (BAC) based on your weight, gender, drinks, and time. Enter your details to see your estimated BAC. Great for safety, planning, or learning about alcohol effects.

  • Understand how alcohol affects your body
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
