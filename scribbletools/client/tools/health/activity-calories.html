About the Activity Calories Calculator

Calculate calories burned for hundreds of activities using scientifically validated MET values. Choose from cardio, strength training, sports, daily activities, and more.

Calorie Burn Factors:
  • Body Weight: Heavier individuals burn more calories
  • Activity Intensity: Higher METs = more calories burned
  • Duration: Longer activities burn more total calories
  • Individual Variation: ±10-20% variation is normal

Use Cases:
  • Track calories for weight management goals
  • Compare calorie burn across different activities
  • Plan exercise routines for specific calorie targets
  • Monitor daily activity and energy expenditure
