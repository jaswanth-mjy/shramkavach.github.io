About the Basal Insulin Estimator

Instantly estimate your daily basal insulin needs based on your weight. Enter your weight to see your estimated basal insulin dose. Great for diabetes management, health, or learning about insulin therapy.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Estimate basal insulin for diabetes management
  • Compare doses for different weights
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
