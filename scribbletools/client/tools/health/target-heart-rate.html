About the Target Heart Rate Finder

Calculate your optimal target heart rate zones for different types of exercise. Uses both simple age-based formula and Karvonen method (if resting HR provided) for more accurate results.

Heart Rate Zones:
  • Fat Burn Zone: 50-60% of max HR - light exercise
  • Aerobic Zone: 60-70% of max HR - moderate exercise
  • Anaerobic Zone: 70-85% of max HR - vigorous exercise
  • Peak Zone: 85-95% of max HR - maximum effort

Use Cases:
  • Optimize cardio workouts for fat burning
  • Train in specific heart rate zones
  • Monitor exercise intensity safely
  • Track fitness progress over time
