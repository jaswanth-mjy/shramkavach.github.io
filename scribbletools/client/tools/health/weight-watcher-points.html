WW Points Calculator

About the WW Points Calculator

Instantly estimate Weight Watchers (WW) points for any food based on calories, fat, and fiber. Enter your values to see the points. Great for meal planning, weight loss, or tracking your nutrition.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Track WW points for foods and meals
  • Plan your daily points for weight loss
  • Compare points for different foods
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
