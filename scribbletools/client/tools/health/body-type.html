About the Body Type Identifier

Instantly estimate your body type (e.g. ectomorph, mesomorph, endomorph, or common shape) using your measurements. Enter your shoulder, waist, and hip measurements to see your likely body type. Great for fitness, health, or personal style.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Identify your body type for fitness or fashion
  • Compare body types for different measurements
  • Set realistic fitness or health goals
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
