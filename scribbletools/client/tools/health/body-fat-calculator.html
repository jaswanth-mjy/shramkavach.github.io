About the Body Fat Percentage Tool

Instantly estimate your body fat percentage using simple measurements. Enter your age, gender, weight, and body measurements to see your estimated body fat. Great for fitness, health, or tracking progress.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Track your body composition for fitness goals
  • Compare body fat for different ages or genders
  • Monitor health and wellness progress
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
