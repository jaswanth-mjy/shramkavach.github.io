Shuffle Lines

The Shuffle Lines tool lets you instantly randomize the order of lines in any block of text. This is perfect for teachers, event organizers, coders, and anyone who needs to mix up lists, questions, or data for fairness, fun, or variety.

  • Randomize Order: Instantly shuffle the order of all lines in your text. Each click produces a new random order.
  • Fair and Unbiased: Great for random draws, assigning tasks, shuffling quiz questions, or mixing up code/data entries.
  • One-Click Simplicity: No setup or configurationâ€”just paste, click, and get your shuffled result.

How to use:

  1. Paste or type your multi-line text into the input box below.
  2. Click Shuffle Lines to instantly randomize the order of all lines.
  3. View the shuffled result in the output area.
  4. Use Copy to copy the result, Download to save as a file, or Clear to reset.

Example uses: Randomize student names for presentations, shuffle contest entries, mix up to-do lists, create randomized practice questions, and more.
