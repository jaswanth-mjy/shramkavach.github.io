Total Surface Area Calculator

About the Total Surface Area Calculator

Instantly find the total surface area (all outside faces) of 3D shapes like cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, and more. Just select your shape, enter the measurements, and see the result. Great for painting, wrapping, or covering objects, or for checking homework and projects.

Use Cases:
  • Calculate the amount of paint or material needed to cover an object
  • Estimate the surface area for packaging or wrapping
  • Check homework or prepare for geometry class
  • Plan craft, construction, or science projects
