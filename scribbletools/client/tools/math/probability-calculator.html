About Probability

Probability: The measure of how likely an event is to occur, expressed as a number between 0 (impossible) and 1 (certain).

Formula:
  • Probability of an event: P(A) = \frac{\text{Number of Favorable Outcomes}}{\text{Total Number of Outcomes}}
Use Cases:
  • Games of chance (dice, cards, coins)
  • Risk assessment and statistics
  • Everyday decision making
