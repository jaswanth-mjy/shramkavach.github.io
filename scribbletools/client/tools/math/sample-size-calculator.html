Sample Size Calculator

About Sample Size Calculation

Sample Size: The number of observations or responses needed to estimate a population parameter with a specified confidence and margin of error. This calculator supports any confidence level from 50% to 99.99%.

Enhanced Features:
  • Any Confidence Level: Supports 50% to 99.99% (not just 90%, 95%, 99%)
  • Precise Z-scores: Uses advanced inverse normal distribution calculations
  • Finite Population Correction: Adjusts for finite population sizes
  • Detailed Results: Shows Z-score, initial and adjusted sample sizes
Formulas:
  • Initial Sample Size (infinite population): n₀ = (z² × p × (1-p)) / e²
  • Adjusted for finite population: n = n₀ / (1 + (n₀-1)/N)
  • Where:
    • z = z-score for confidence level (calculated precisely)
    • p = estimated proportion (as decimal, e.g. 0.5)
    • e = margin of error (as decimal, e.g. 0.05 for 5%)
    • N = population size
Use Cases:
  • Survey and poll design
  • Scientific and medical research
  • Quality control and business analytics
