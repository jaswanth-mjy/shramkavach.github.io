About the Sphere Calculator

Calculate all properties of a sphere from any known value: radius, diameter, surface area, or volume.

Formulas:
  • Surface Area: A = 4πr²
  • Volume: V = (4/3)πr³
  • Diameter: d = 2r
  • Circumference of great circle: C = 2πr
