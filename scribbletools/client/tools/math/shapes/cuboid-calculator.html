About the Cuboid Calculator

Calculate volume and surface areas of a cuboid (rectangular prism) from its three dimensions.

Formulas:
  • Volume: V = length × width × height
  • Total Surface Area: TSA = 2(lw + wh + hl)
  • Lateral Surface Area: LSA = 2h(l + w)
  • Space Diagonal: d = √(l² + w² + h²)
Use Cases:
  • Container and packaging calculations
  • Room volume and wall area calculations
  • Material estimation for boxes and tanks
  • Engineering and architectural planning
