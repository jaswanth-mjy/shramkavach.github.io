As dusk settles over the Canton of Grisons, Davos-Klosters has transformed from a ski resort into a heavily fortified geopolitical command center. The decision to forgo heavy policy speeches on the eve of the summit in favor of a "musical concert" reflects a psychological understanding of the attendees' fatigue.

The Evening's Program

The opening night is dedicated to setting a "welcoming mood," a tactical softening of the environment. This serves as an auditory metaphor for the forum's goal: harmonizing the dissonant interests of the Global North and South.
