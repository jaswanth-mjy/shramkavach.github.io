About Gold Prices in India

The price of gold in India is influenced by various factors including international gold prices, currency exchange rates (USD to INR), import duties, and GST. Gold rates vary across different Indian cities due to local taxes, transportation costs, and regional demand.

24 Karat Gold: 24K gold is 99.9% pure gold and is considered the purest form. It's softer and primarily used for investment purposes like gold coins and bars.

22 Karat Gold: 22K gold contains 91.6% pure gold mixed with other metals for durability. It's the most popular choice for jewelry in India.

18 Karat Gold: 18K gold has 75% gold content and is commonly used in designer and lightweight jewelry.
