The "Productivity Paradox" Persists

While AI tools reliably increase individual task speed, organizational throughput often remains stagnant because bottlenecks have migrated from "writing code" to "reviewing, testing, and deploying". Engineering leaders increasingly value "judgment and systems thinking" over raw output. By 2026, effectiveness is assessed based on creativity and innovation rather than velocity or lines of code.
